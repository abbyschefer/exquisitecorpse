What's at the end of the Rainbow?

Rainbows are known as a sign of good luck, or an omen of better things to come, universally understood around the world. But what should happen if you came across one, tangible in front of you, that you could reach out and touch? There's no leprechaun at the start, no pot of gold to entice you to move forward. Taking one tentative step onto its multicolored surface, you're surprised to feel that it supports your weight, and are compelled to keep walking forward. The ground is far below your feet, but you're determined to see what may lie at the end of the rainbow.

You have found yourself at the foot of this rainbow. You gaze up in disbelief, unsure of whether or not you should attempt to cross it. This must be the sign of good luck you have been waiting for all your life. You reach out and touches the rainbow, convincing yourself that it is real. You begin to climb to the top of it's arch, taking careful steps as you are unsure of how long the rainbow will last. You stand at the top and gaze in awe at the Earth beneath you. Suddenly, everything turns black.

As the black atmosphere begins to engulf all parts of the rainbow, you begin to reach for any signs of luck that the rainbow once represented. Your steps become smaller and smaller and suddenly you can no longer hear the sounds of the earth that were once so loud. In this momemnt, you begin to feel hopeless as there is no way you will be able to find the beginning of the rainbow once again. Fear begins to creep in, but instead of letting the fear engulf you like the black surroundings did the rainbow, you choose to sit as still as possible. And for the first time the darkness is no longer scary and the silence isn't deafening. You accept what must be your fate but then suddenly you smell something familiar...

The scent is familiar, but nothing you have ever smelled before. It reminds you of the world when there was light, but the darkness still surrounds you. You squeeze your eyes closed to envision the scent and your mind becomes filled with color. You realize the smell is red, yellow, green, and blue. You envision a pink tulip and can smell the color pink. You focus harder and realize this color also has a song. It sounds as you would imagine to be sung by a beautiful fairy. You open your eyes to return to the darkness, but the darkness is gone. Your seat has relocated to a place of tranquility. You are calm and surrounded by color. The end of the rainbow was no pot of gold or leperchaun, it was instead you finding your inner peace.
